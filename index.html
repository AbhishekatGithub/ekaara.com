Our Story

Born from emotion,
built on legacy

Ekaara is more than a jewelry brand. It is a living canvas of emotion — where every piece tells a story of craft, culture, and connection.

Our Journey

The Ekaara timeline

2024

The Idea is Born

Ekaara begins as a vision — to create a jewelry brand that speaks the language of emotion. Rooted in South Indian heritage, designed for the modern Indian woman.

2025

The Three Collections Take Shape

Signature by Nishta, Statement by Kiyara, and Soul by Vedaara are conceptualised. Artisan partnerships forged across Nagercoil, Thanjavur and Karimnagar.

2026

First Sales & Community

The debut collection launches. First customers. First stories. The Ekaara community begins — real women, real emotions, real jewelry.

2027

The Movement Grows

AI design configurator, offline boutique, Artisan Revival Programme. Ekaara becomes a movement of meaning, not just a product company.

What We Stand For

Our values

Heritage First

We draw from centuries of South Indian goldsmithing — temple jewelry, filigree, coin work — and translate it for the modern everyday.

Emotion-Led Design

Every SKU begins with a feeling, not a trend. We ask: what emotion does this piece carry? What memory will it hold?

Artisan Dignity

Our craftspeople are not vendors. They are co-creators. We pay fairly, credit openly, and build with them — not just from them.

Accessible Luxury

Fine craftsmanship should not cost a fortune. Ekaara brings heirloom-quality pieces to the ₹1,000–₹5,000 range.

Sustainability

Recycled silver initiative, responsible gemstone sourcing, minimal packaging waste. We build for the future.

Community

Ekaara is built by its wearers. Customer stories, gifting moments, and personal milestones are the heart of the brand.

Behind the Craft

The Making Of

From raw material to your hands — the sacred journey of every Ekaara piece.

The Process

How every piece comes alive

01
Design & Emotion

Every piece starts with a feeling. Our designers draw from South Indian heritage motifs — temple carvings, nature, mythology — and translate them into contemporary wearable forms.

02
Artisan Crafting

Skilled goldsmiths and silver artisans from Nagercoil, Thanjavur and Karimnagar bring designs to life using techniques passed down through generations — lost wax casting, filigree, hand-engraving.

03
Gemstone Setting

For the Soul by Vedaara collection, gemstones are sourced responsibly — amethyst, lapis lazuli, rose quartz, rudraksha — and set with care to preserve their natural energy.

04
Finish & Quality Check

Each piece undergoes a rigorous quality check — plating thickness, clasp integrity, stone setting, surface finish. Only pieces that meet the Ekaara standard leave the workshop.

Our Craftspeople

The hands behind Ekaara

The Nagercoil Silversmiths
Tamil Nadu · Silver Filigree

Masters of delicate silver filigree — a tradition in coastal Tamil Nadu for over 200 years. They craft the fine thread-work pieces in the Signature by Nishta collection.

The Thanjavur Temple Jewelers
Tamil Nadu · Temple Jewelry

Generations of craftspeople who have supplied temple jewelry to Dravidian shrines across South India. Their gold-plated work forms the soul of our heritage collections.

The Karimnagar Craftsmen
Telangana · Silver Work

Known for bold silver jewelry with Deccan influences, the Karimnagar artisans bring a distinctive energy to the Statement by Kiyara and Soul by Vedaara lines.
